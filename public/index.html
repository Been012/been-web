About Me

I'm Ben McPherson.

I work in cybersecurity and spend the rest of my time interested in things that have nothing to do with cybersecurity — fungi, plants, game development, DIY, 3D Printing and whatever AI is getting up to lately.

I graduated in Computer Networks & Security and have built a career in the field since. Outside of that, I'm usually found doing something with my hands or my head: tinkering with a game in Godot, identifying a mushroom, or going down a rabbit hole I didn't intend to.

This site is many things: a portfolio, a notebook, a creative outlet. It's also a bit of a personal milestone. Growing up in the early 2000s, owning a domain with your own name felt like something reserved for celebrities and corporations. The idea that I'd one day have benmcpherson.co.uk — built by my own hands — would have seemed impossible to a younger me.

This page is something of an ode to my dad — for fostering every odd interest I ever had, and for believing I'd get here long before I did.

Have a look around. There's writing, projects, and probably a few things that don't fit neatly into a category. That feels about right.
